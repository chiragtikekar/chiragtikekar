About Me

Hola, I'm Chirag!

As a child, I was captivated by movies like Wall-E and Big Hero 6. Their imaginative worlds and the way technology played such an integral role in shaping those stories ignited a curiosity in me. I remember watching Wall-E’s journey through space and thinking, "What if we could build something like that?" These films planted the seed of fascination with engineering and the endless possibilities technology could bring to life.

Now, as a student pursuing a Bachelors in Computer Science & Engineering at SAL Institute of Technology & Engineering Research under the Gujarat Technological University, that curiosity has grown into a passion for creating something new and innovating.

I have also participated in some programs other than academics, like Nirma University’s HackNUthon Event a 36 hour long Hackathon,Introduction to Database a certificate course by Meta, Geo-data sharing and Cyber Security Course by the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing - Indian Space Research Organisation (IIRS-ISRO) Outreach Programe.

Looking ahead, I want to push my skills further, with the goal of working on projects that are challenging and truly make a positive difference.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me watching movies, exploring new programming languages, or keeping up with the latest in tech trends.
